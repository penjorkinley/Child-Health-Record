About Us

Welcome

It's true that a healthy child requires a healthy mother. A child cannot be healthy or happy unless his or her mother is healthy and happy. Maternal health during pregnancy and after delivery is essential for a mother's physical and mental well-being, as well as her ability to provide loving, proper care to her newborn child at birth and in the years after.

Since mothers play such a crucial role in their children's health and quality of life, it's critical to prioritize maternal health during pregnancy. The majority of pregnant women concentrate on the growth and wellbeing of their unborn child. Expectant moms should, however, have regular prenatal visits not just to assure the health of their kid, but also to ensure their own health both during and after pregnancy.

Any current health hazards can be detected and steps made to prevent any continued health problems not just for the kid but also for Mom during her pregnancy and prenatal care appointments.
